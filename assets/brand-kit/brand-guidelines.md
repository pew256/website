# pew256 Brand Guidelines

## Brand Story
pew256 translates complex decentralized architectures into actionable enterprise roadmaps. Our identity reflects our core value: acting as a resilient, precision-engineered bridge between legacy systems and the digital assets future.

## Master Logo
Our master logo combines the "Resilient Bridge" icon with precise, modern typography.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Slate Blue:** `#506684` - The cornerstone of our brand, conveying institutional trust, security, and enterprise readiness.
- **Green:** `#2E8B57` - Reflects growth, success, and the proactive adoption of new financial paradigms.
- **White (Knockout):** `#FFFFFF` - Used for maximum contrast and readability on dark and vibrant backgrounds.

## Typography

### Display / Headings
- **Font Family:** Montserrat
- **Usage:** Used for titles, the logo mark, and primary messaging statements to create a commanding and contemporary presence.

### Body Text
- **Font Family:** Lato
- **Usage:** Applied to body copy and secondary information for exceptional legibility and an approachable feel.

## Asset Usage
- Use the **primary (slate-blue)** logo on light backgrounds for maximum impact and clarity.
- Ensure adequate spacing around the logo to preserve its visual integrity. Use at least 25% of the logo's height as clear space.
- The **standalone icon** is recommended for avatars, social media profiles, and situations where space is heavily constrained.
